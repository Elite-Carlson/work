English

Learn english with Befa


English is an official language in 67 countries and continously growing in size when it comes to english speakers, English is one of the most widely used languages in the world while also being one of the most critical and in demand languges in places such as school's and jobs.

Befa language school strives to help you expand your vocabulary with years of experiance befa is here to help you reach your goal.

Our aim is to help you learn English and improve your communication skills by providing you with fun, interesting and interactive lessons that will help you develop your speaking, listening and reading skills in an environment which encourages learning.




What do i need to start?


You can start from wherever you want - whether that's having just started learning English or being a more advanced learner. You don't need any previous knowledge of the language to get started; all we ask is that you're willing to work hard!


Contact Us 😊



Why learn English?

The main benefit of learning English at befa language school is that it will improve your communication skills and your social life. You will also have access to free audio-visual materials for your classes.

Befa language school is a private language school that offers English as a Second Language (ESL) courses in the city of Kigali. The school was founded by Theoneste and has been operating since 2008. It is located in Kigali Rwanda with a number of different locations around the country.
