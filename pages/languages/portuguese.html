Start Learning Portuguese

Learn Portuguese with Befa


Portuguese is a Romance language that originated in the north of Portugal, Spoken by approximately 90 million people in Portugal and Brazil. Portuguese has many similarities with Spanish, but it is not the same language as Spanish.The basic grammar rules are very similar to those of English, with some minor differences in spelling and pronunciation.

It is spoken primarily in Brazil and Portugal, but also throughout most of Africa (especially Angola), East Timor, Macau, Mozambique and Goa. In addition to being an official language in both countries it has been widely used as a lingua franca among other former Portuguese colonies such as Cape Verde (in the African continent) and São Tomé e Príncipe (in Central America). The main dialects are Brazilian Portuguese or European Portuguese and Angolan Portuguese.




What do i need to start?


You can start from wherever you want - whether that's having just started learning Portuguese or being a more advanced learner. You don't need any previous knowledge of the language to get started; all we ask is that you're willing to work hard!

Why learn Portuguese?

Portuguese is a Romance language spoken in Portugal, Brazil and some other countries. It is the second most widely-spoken language in the Americas after Spanish. The Portuguese language has been influenced by many different cultures over the centuries, including Arabic, Italian, French and English.

As such it has developed its own unique grammar and vocabulary that are very difficult to master for those who do not know any Portuguese at all. Our course will teach you how to speak basic phrases of Portuguese so that you can communicate with locals when traveling or living abroad.
