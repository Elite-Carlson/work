Start Learning Spanish

Learn Spanish with Befa


Spanish is the official language of Spain and a co-official language in Equatorial Guinea, Mexico, Central America and Venezuela. It is also one of the two working languages of the European Union (the other being English). In terms of native speakers, Spanish is spoken by about 400 million people worldwide.

We help you learn Spanish in an easy and fun way. Our method has been designed to be the easiest way for beginners to learn Spanish, but it can also be used by intermediate learners as well. You can choose from five different levels of difficulty: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, Fluency and Super-Advanced. Each level has its own unique set of lessons that focus on specific areas of the language such as grammar or vocabulary. We are constantly adding new content so make sure you check back often!




What do i need to start?


You can start from wherever you want - whether that's having just started learning Spanish or being a more advanced learner. You don't need any previous knowledge of the language to get started; all we ask is that you're willing to work hard!

Why learn with Befa?

Spanish is one of the most widely spoken languages in the world and it will be very useful to know it if you want to travel around Latin America, Spain, Mexico or any other country where there are many Spaniards living. Learning Spanish will also help you communicate with your family members back home as well as make new friends from all over the world!
