Start Learning Mandarin Chinese

Learn Chinese with Befa


Mandarin is a language spoken in China. It is the most widely used Chinese dialect outside of Mainland China, and it is also one of the official languages of Singapore. Mandarin was formerly known as “Putonghua” (common speech).

it's also one of the most spoken languages in the world. In fact, there are more than 1 billion speakers worldwide! In general, mandarin is used by people who speak different dialects of Chinese - such as Cantonese.

It's most widely spoken today in Taiwan, where it's called “Hokkien.” Mandarin is also used as a lingua franca throughout much of East Asia, and both the Chinese government and some people use it as their primary language.




What do i need to start?


You can start from wherever you want - whether that's having just started learning Chinese or being a more advanced learner. You don't need any previous knowledge of the language to get started; all we ask is that you're willing to work hard!

Why learn Mandarin?

Mandarin has been called "the language of the future" because it is being adopted by people all over the world, especially in business. The number of native Mandarin speakers will continue to grow as more people learn to speak it fluently, but there are still many who do not know how to speak it well enough for everyday use.
