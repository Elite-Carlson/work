Kids and Teens

Kids Language Lessons


Kids language lessons are for children who have a difficult time learning to speak, read or write. The program is structured in an easy-to-follow manner with fun games and songs that will help your child learn the basics of language.

Teens language lessons are a great way to introduce your child to the world of foreign languages. You can choose from over 40 different languages, and as you progress through each lesson, you will learn new vocabulary, grammar rules and sentence structure.




Spanish for kids and teens

Spanish is spoken by about 400 million people worldwide. Spanish is the official language of Spain and a co-official language in Equatorial Guinea, Mexico, Central America and Venezuela. It is also one of the two working languages of the European Union (the other being English). With this In mind Spanish will come in very handy in developing your kid's or teeangers brain and coomunication skill's while also opening a number of different future opportunities to them.




French for kids and teens

French is a Romance language spoken by about 65 million people in France and some other French-speaking countries.French is the most widely spoken language in Europe. It is an official language of France, Belgium and Switzerland and has a number of regional dialects. It is an official language of the European Union, as well as being one of the six working languages of the United Nations.
