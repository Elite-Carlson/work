Our Goverment Program


Government language learning is the study of a foreign language for use in government. It may be used in diplomacy and international relations, or it may be used for official business within a country's borders.

This is not to be mistaken withcorporation language learning where by the employer funds the education of the employee, The goverment language learnig is only availale for Goverments and their personel, Please contact us to see wich plan is most suitable for your deparment as each goverment is different in it's own way.
